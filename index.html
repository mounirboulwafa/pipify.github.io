Floating Player – PiPify

Floating Player – PiPify is an iOS utility app that allows you to watch video content in a floating Picture-in-Picture (PiP) window while using other apps.

The app is designed for multitasking and productivity, enabling continuous playback in a movable floating window across your device.

Key Features

  • Picture-in-Picture video playback
  • Floating video window for multitasking
  • Background playback support
  • Queue and favorites management
  • Clipboard and share-based video loading

Content Disclaimer

Floating Player – PiPify does not host, store, or provide any video content. All videos are accessed directly from third-party websites chosen by the user.

Floating Player – PiPify is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by YouTube or any other third-party content provider.

Privacy

The app does not collect personal data. Any app preferences or settings are stored locally on your device only.

Support
